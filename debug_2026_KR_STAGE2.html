    Format

    [edit]
    • Regular Season: June 5th - July 12th
      • Round Robin
      • All matches are Ft3
      • Top 4 teams advance to Playoffs Seeding Decider Matches
      • Next 4 teams advance to Last Chance Qualifier
      • Bottom team proceed to promotion/relegation for Stage 3
    • Playoffs Seeding Decider Matches: July 3th - 5th
      • Round robin
      • All matches are Ft3
      • All teams advance to Regional Playoffs
      • Top 2 teams receive a first-round bye
    • Last Chance Qualifier: July 3th - 5th
      • Double elimination bracket
      • All matches are Ft3
      • 5th and 6th team from Regular Season start from Upper Bracket
      • Top 2 teams advance to Regional Playoffs
      • Bottom 2 teams proceed to promotion/relegation for Stage 3
    • Regional Playoffs: July 10st - 12rd
      • Single elimination bracket
      • All matches are Ft4
      • Top 2 teams advance to Midseason Championship
    • Hero Bans
      • Each team bans 1 hero per map, with the ban impacting both teams
      • Each ban must be from a different role than the other team's ban
      • Teams can only ban a hero once per series, but may repeat a ban from the opposing team
      • The higher seeded team or team that has lost the last map chooses to ban first or second
